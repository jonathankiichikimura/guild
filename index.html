Description

Guild is a concept for a web app that reframes everyday help as a quest-based marketplace.

Users post simple help requests as “quests,” while others browse, accept, and complete them—prioritizing simplicity, affordability, and human connection.

Target

Guild will focus on tech-literate older adults who need help with everyday tasks.

The secondary target will be the younger community members who want flexible ways to contribute, earn small rewards, and build connections across generations.

Pain

Many older adults living independently will face challenges accessing help for everyday tasks, especially if family members live far away.

Simple errands or household chores may feel difficult or awkward to request from others. Existing options will often require professional or formal services, which can make even small tasks costly and impersonal.

This gap will leave people without an easy, approachable way to get the support they need. As a result, both convenience and social connection will be limited for these individuals.

Solution

Guild will be a web app which reimagines everyday help as simple, task-based exchanges. Users will post small help requests as missions on a shared board, where others can browse, accept, and complete them.

By structuring interactions in a clear and approachable way, Guild will make it easy for older adults to request help without feeling awkward.

The platform will also provide opportunities for younger community members to contribute, earn small rewards, and build meaningful connections across generations. Ultimately, Guild aims to make everyday support more accessible, human, and community-driven than traditional formal services.

Features

What I have envisioned now for a 2-3 week prototype:

  • SMTP Notification System
  • Geolocation Tracking
  • Progress Badge System
  • Requester:Accepter Messaging UI
  • Location Based Filtering
